DAYS OF ADVENTURE

Rafting trips along the Colorado River through the Grand Canyon can vary from single-day excursions to multi-day expeditions lasting up to three weeks, depending on the chosen route and tour operator.

Information of the Place

The Grand Canyon offers one of the most iconic and awe-inspiring rafting experiences in the world. Rafters can navigate through thrilling rapids, explore hidden side canyons, and camp under the stars amidst the canyon's breathtaking scenery.

Benefits

  • Majestic Scenery: Rafting through the Grand Canyon provides unparalleled views of towering cliffs, ancient rock formations, and the mesmerizing colors of the canyon walls.
  • Diverse Rapids: The Colorado River offers a mix of rapids, ranging from exciting Class III to heart-pounding Class V, providing an adrenaline-packed adventure for rafters of all skill levels.
  • Cultural Insights: Rafting in the Grand Canyon allows for opportunities to learn about the area's rich indigenous history, geological wonders, and diverse ecosystem.

Price: $400

DAYS OF ADVENTURE

Rafting trips on the American River typically range from half-day excursions to full-day adventures, with options available for both beginners and experienced rafters.

Information of the Place

The American River, located in Northern California's Gold Country, offers thrilling rafting experiences amidst scenic landscapes, historic mining towns, and abundant wildlife.

Benefits

  • Close Proximity: The American River is easily accessible from major cities like Sacramento and San Francisco, making it a convenient option for a day trip or weekend getaway.
  • Varied Difficulty Levels: The river features sections with rapids suitable for everyone, from families with children to adrenaline-seeking thrill-seekers, ensuring there's something for everyone.
  • Scenic Beauty: Rafting on the American River provides stunning views of the Sierra Nevada foothills, oak-studded hillsides, and crystal-clear waters, creating a picturesque backdrop for outdoor adventures.

Price: $200

DAYS OF ADVENTURE

Rafting trips on the Snake River can vary from half-day excursions to full-day adventures, with options available for scenic float trips or adrenaline-pumping whitewater rafting experiences.

Information of the Place

The Snake River flows through majestic landscapes, including Grand Teton National Park and Jackson Hole Valley, offering stunning views of snow-capped mountains, abundant wildlife, and pristine wilderness.

Benefits

  • Scenic Splendor: Rafting on the Snake River provides unparalleled views of the Teton Range, including the iconic peaks of Grand Teton and Mount Moran, creating a picturesque backdrop for outdoor adventures.
  • Wildlife Encounters: The Snake River is home to a diverse array of wildlife, including bald eagles, ospreys, moose, elk, and beavers, offering opportunities for wildlife spotting during rafting trips.
  • Varied Rapids: The river features sections with Class II to Class IV rapids, providing options for both beginner-friendly float trips and thrilling whitewater rafting adventures, catering to a wide range of preferences.

Price: $500
